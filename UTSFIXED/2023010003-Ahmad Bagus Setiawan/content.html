eFootball Tips and Strategies

1. Master the Controls

Familiarize yourself with the controls for dribbling, passing, and shooting. Precision is key in eFootball.

2. Build a Balanced Team

Focus on creating a team with strong defense, midfield, and attackers. Balance is more important than individual skill.

3. Use Formations Effectively

Experiment with formations like 4-3-3 or 4-4-2 to find the one that fits your playstyle.

4. Practice Set Pieces

Corner kicks and free kicks can be game-changers. Spend time mastering these skills.
